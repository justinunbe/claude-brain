# Life Framework

## The 5 Areas

All five are always "on" — none can be ignored. But only one gets primary focus at a time (Tortoise & Hare).

- **Health** — Living within a healthy range for strength, cardio, diet, flexibility, and sleep
- **Wealth** — Enough money to live autonomously and choose how to spend time
- **Relationships** — People close in life where there's give/receive of trust, love, connection
- **Purpose** — Making an impact in something that personally matters
- **Legacy** — The continuation of influence through what's passed on to others

## The Tortoise & Hare Method

All five move forward always. One is the "hare" (active goal). The other four are "tortoises" (maintenance). Strong active goals only get set around the hare. When the hare's goal is hit, focus switches.

## Core Values (innermost)

Peace, Impact, Vitality, Autonomy, Love, Influence.

## Path to Fulfillment (Jay's diagram)

Areas → Specific Interests → Organization → (Discipline + Communication) → Values + Beliefs → Peace → Core Values → Fulfillment.

## Base skills by Area

- **Health** = Discipline
- **Relationships** = Communication
- **Wealth** = Discipline + Communication

## The 3 master skills (meta)

Organization, Discipline, Communication. These unlock everything else.

- **Organization** — planning, next-step selection, adapting, tracking
- **Discipline** — doing the things, flow, deep work
- **Communication** — alignment, outward to people and AI

## Ownership model (how Jay and the AI partner work together)

**Jay owns:**
1. Vision — what, high-level how, why it matters
2. Standards — what finished/winning looks like
3. Exceptions — when something falls outside the regular standards

**AI owns:**
1. Process — the best way to solve the vision being asked
2. Execution — running the process to the finish line
3. Iteration — improving to better meet vision/standards
4. Process decisions — unless Jay proactively overrides

**Shared (deep work):**
Whoever can actually do it owns it. Machine tasks default to AI (file structure, Git, plumbing, orchestration). Vision, physical training, hard conversations, and thinking are Jay's.
