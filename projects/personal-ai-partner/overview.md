# Personal AI Partner

## What

Build a personal AI partner/assistant that solves for organization, discipline, and communication across all areas of Jay's life. Not a product to sell — a system Jay uses for himself.

## Why

Session 4 (2026-04-20): After hunting SaaS categories and rejecting all of them (too boring, can't fake interest), Jay reframed. Not searching for a product right now. Instead: build the best possible personal AI partner, use it for months, then make the next product decision from a much stronger foundation.

The three master skills (Organization, Discipline, Communication) are both Jay's weaknesses and the problems that interest him most. Building for himself first.

## Design philosophy

- **Tony Stark / Jarvis** — in-tandem work, not chatbox back-and-forth. AI as invested partner.
- **Interface is the missing piece** — robot working alongside, not 10 minutes of chat-and-wait.
- **Discipline companion** — suffering alone is hard; suffering with someone is funny.
- **From future regret backward** — no existing product starts from "I wish I had..." and works backward to today.

## Current phase

**Phase 1: Foundation** — building the memory architecture (this repo). In progress.
