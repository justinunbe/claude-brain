### Idea #2 — Sunscreen Back Applicator
**Date:** 2026-04-16
**Interest:** 3/10

**Core insight:** Applying sunscreen to your own back is genuinely hard, even with long-handle tools (15-20 min). Two problems: can't reach efficiently, can't see coverage. Full solution = physical applicator + color-indicator formula.

### Idea #1 — Fitness Consistency App ("One More Day")
**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Interest:** 6/10

**Core insight:** Consistency failure in fitness is not a motivation problem — it's a cascade failure problem. One external disruption creates a crack. Stress compounds. The "what the hell" moment hits — once someone decides the day is lost, they stop optimizing. One missed workout → missed day → written-off week.

Most apps try to prevent the first miss. Nobody is built for what happens AFTER. The miss isn't the problem — the spiral is.

**The philosophy:** "One more day" — not a tagline, an architecture. The product doesn't care that you missed yesterday. It only cares that you show up today. No streaks to break. No failure state.

**Target user:** Anyone with a full life and competing pressures — busy professionals, parents, anyone juggling real responsibilities.

**Differentiator:** Every competitor prevents the miss (reminders, streaks, gamification). This is built around frictionless re-entry.

**Open questions:**
- What does "one more day" look like as a product feature?
- How does it detect/respond to cascade patterns?
- Physical component or pure software?
- Monetization?
