# Session 2 — Wealth Deep Dive

## Values surfaced

- Struggle is the "what," not the "how" — confident in execution.
- Keeps landing on meta ideas (teach habits, life coaching) but rejects it — doesn't want to add noise, believes people should forge their own path.
- Not a social media person — against scrolling/numbing culture, uses YouTube for learning only.

## Core driver

Purpose and impact. Admires Musk's model — every business solves something real and critical. Doesn't need to be that big, but wants what he does to truly matter.

## What doesn't motivate him

Quick money. Rat race (bigger house, car). Status.

## What does motivate him

Autonomy and safety from wealth — not status. Genuine impact.

## Key tension

Always gets stuck because the impact/purpose filter is a very high bar to clear.
