# Session 4 — Business Hunting + Personal AI OS Decision

## Ideas logged

None — deliberate pause on product search.

## Key work done

### Hunted SaaS categories
Presented 10 proven categories using Mike's 10-step playbook criteria (CS tools, client portals, SOP builders, etc.). Jay rejected all — too boring. Confirmed: he can't fake interest, grit doesn't work for him, he only commits to things he genuinely cares about.

### Pivoted to fitness/health consumer
Jay's health stack is already well-solved: Workouts by MacroFactor (lifting), C2 rower, walking treadmill + Apple Watch, MacroFactor (diet). No burning unsolved problem.

### Consumer vs B2B tension
Jay wants to sell to health-conscious consumers (he IS this person) but AppSumo/LTD model is B2B. Real fork surfaced.

### Design philosophy named
Jay's NOT excited by product categories — excited by the HOW. Zappos as example: eliminated the dread of customer service. "The way you do anything is the way you do everything." Build a brand where experience is so obsessively good that people would follow it into any category.

### Builder/visionary archetype confirmed
NOT a promoter/marketer. Needs PLG baked into product, not bolted on. Loom/Slack/Calendly model: product spreads by being used.

### Design pillars articulated
**Consistency, Specific Knowledge, Progress** = Organization, Discipline, Communication. The problems that interest Jay AND his personal weaknesses.

### Tony Stark AI partner vision deepened
In-tandem work (not chatbox back-and-forth). AI as invested partner for weekly planning — "sign the kids up for swim lessons" → AI does 90% → Jay takes to finish line. Discipline companion: suffering alone is hard, suffering with someone is funny. Missing piece = the INTERFACE.

### "I wish I had..." pain point
Deepest suffering Jay identified. Regret surfaces at the collision moment — pool, house, book, conversation. Work to avoid those happens years earlier, but urgency only arrives at the moment — too late. No product starts from future regret and works backward to today.

## Decision

Jay is NOT pursuing a product right now. Will spend the coming months building the best possible personal AI assistant/PA for himself. Once truly useful, next product decision comes from a much stronger foundation.
